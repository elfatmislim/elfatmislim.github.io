Tutto Novo: A Brand Identity and Visual System for Tutto Sport

Slim El Fatmi · October 18, 2025 · 8 min read
Key Insights
  • Tutto Sport's problem is not product or distribution. It is identity. The brand has stores and inventory but no coherent reason for a customer to prefer it over Peak or Decathlon.
  • The Italian tennis heritage angle is a genuine white space in Tunisian sportswear. Nobody is owning Mediterranean elegance. That gap is an asset, but only if claimed with consistency.
  • A visual system only works when every execution is disciplined. One discount video shot on a phone in bad lighting erases months of brand-building. Execution is the real competitive advantage.

Tutto Sport is a Tunisian sneaker and sportswear reseller with a real problem. Not a product problem. Not a distribution problem. A brand problem. The stores exist, the inventory exists, the price points are competitive. What does not exist is a coherent reason for a customer to prefer Tutto Sport over the increasingly confident alternatives crowding the same space.

This document is a brand identity proposal called Tutto Novo. It channels the golden era of Italian tennis, specifically the 1970s and 1980s, through a modern American streetwear lens. The result is a brand where sport becomes poetry, performance meets style, and the Mediterranean spirit runs through everything from the packaging to the photography to the copy on a window display.

In one line: effortlessly cool. Not try-hard cool. Not discount cool. The kind of cool that looks like it was not trying, which of course requires more effort than anything else.

Brand Pillars

Four pillars hold the identity together. Each one references something real, something with cultural weight, something that a Tunisian customer in 2025 can feel even if they cannot name the reference.

01

Heritage and Place

  • Italian tennis golden age (Adriano Panatta, 70s Rome)
  • Mediterranean lifestyle and light
  • Historic courts of Rome, Florence, Milano
  • Italian basketball courts and running trails
02

Sport as Theater

  • Performance is elegance in motion
  • The court is a stage, not just competition
  • Sport should feel poetic, not painful
  • Every movement is worth watching
03

Style Fusion

  • 80s surf culture energy
  • American streetwear modernity
  • Italian old money tennis aesthetic
  • Athletic wear (Fila, Ellesse heritage), not luxury
04

Attitude

  • Bold, poetic, funny
  • Effortlessly cool archetype
  • Sunny, vibrant, never boring
  • Bling bling with friends energy

Visual Identity

The design philosophy is minimalist but bold. Empty space that feels intentional. Every image should look like a painting bathed in golden Mediterranean sunlight. The aesthetic equation: Nike meets old money Italian tennis courts. Wooden rackets meet Fila stripes meet modern streetwear. 70s Italian elegance meets 80s surf coolness.

Color Palette

Every color is a reference to something real. The sky above a clay court in Rome. The yellow of a new tennis ball at the start of a set. The green of turf at golden hour. The palette must feel warm, confident, and Mediterranean at all times.

Baseline BlueLogo, typography, key signage. Italian sky, court confidence.
Tennis BallEnergy accents, call-to-actions. Vibrant sport energy.
Serve and TurfSophistication highlights. Grounded elegance.
Center Court FogBackgrounds, web UI. Early morning light, simplicity.
Drop Shot MintSeasonal highlights, surf nods. Fresh, vibrant 80s summer.
Aqua AdvantagePackaging, lifestyle visuals. Coastal ease, Mediterranean breeze.

Typography and Photography

Primary fontHelvetica Neue Bold. Clean, modern, athletic. Strong enough to carry minimalist compositions. Timeless like the sport itself.
LightingGolden hour always. Minimalist framing with intentional empty space. Sunny, breezy, effortless.
SubjectsClay courts in Rome, Italian architecture, basketball in nature, modern athletes in 80s-inspired athletic streetwear.
MoodLa Belle Epoque tennis atmosphere. Mediterranean warmth. Theater of sport. Bling bling with friends energy.

Brand Personality and Voice

The brand archetype is the effortlessly cool guide. Bold, poetic, funny. Confident without arrogance. Makes sport feel inspiring rather than exhausting. The voice is Italian in spirit, Mediterranean in warmth, and streetwear in energy.

Voice lines"Sportivo, stile, sempre." / "Elegance in motion." / "Sport is poetry."
MissionMake sport cool, fun, and elegant. Never boring or painful. Position Tutto Sport as the guide to being effortlessly cool in sport and in life.
Layout ruleEmbrace emptiness. Negative space is a design element. Always sunny, bright, optimistic, golden. Think gallery, not catalog.
StripesUse sparingly but confidently. Blue/white and green/white. They carry the Italian tennis court into every execution.

Tutto Sport is where Italian elegance meets American streetwear energy, where the court becomes a canvas, and where every sneaker tells a story of sun, style, and the effortless poetry of movement. The brand exists at the intersection of a place, an era, and an attitude. The question is not whether this identity could work. The question is whether Tutto Sport has the execution discipline to hold it consistently, or whether the next discount video will undo it in 30 seconds.
